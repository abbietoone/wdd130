Overview

Purpose

Build Revenue by inspiring people to go white water rafting

Audience

Tourists who want to have an adventure while on vacation. People who are seeking to experience the beauty and not just see it from afar. They want to ba able to go with their whole vacation group; this includes younger children and families. They want an adventure but they also want to be safe. This isn't risking your life, this is enhancing your life with fun experiences that everyone will remember.
