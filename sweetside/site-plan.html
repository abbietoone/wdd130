Shop

Freeze-dried candy allows your imagination to run wild. Imagine a gummy worm the texture of a Cheeto puff or a Jolly Rancher that dissolves in your mouth in the first three seconds, these amazing Sensations happen when you take all of your favorite classic candies and freeze-dry them. When you freeze dry candy it expands and becomes crunchy. The moisture is drawn out in the process and you are left with a crunchy and fluffy new version of your favorite treat. Check out all of our flavors. The same classic flavor but with a new name.

The all-time fan favorite is the original flavors of Skittles. We also carry wildberry and sour skiddles. The runner-up flavor is the nerdy clusters, these are freeze-dried gummy nerds. It should be gummy candy with a crunch on the inside becomes a very fluffy treat. Be warned, products are very delicious!

About Us

We started our family-owned business in March of 2023 as a means to help me as a 17-year-old save money for college. The vision for the initial business changed multiple times as we figured out where we best fit. We learned that in order to sell online we would have to get a commercial kitchen so instead we started with events and then approached brick-and-mortar stores and locations to sell there. We did pop-up events in local coffee shops and sold on social media. We started in a couple of boutiques and music stores but eventually found our niche. We are now located in six different stores across the Phoenix metropolitan area. We encourage people to shop local and support small businesses.
